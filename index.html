About

After an engineering degree (B.Sc and M.Sc) in computer science at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, EPFL, passion and curiosity drove me to sucessfully conduct an industrial PhD program at InterDigital, Inc., in collaboration with VirtUs team at Inria, Université de Rennes, CNRS, IRISA. Throughout this work, I explored state-of-the-art deep learning techniques in the field of character animation for content production. I am currently looking for new challenges and opportunities. My taste for learning and solving problems makes me open to various positions including engineer or researcher roles in R&D and to discover new fields.

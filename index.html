About

I'm currently conducting an industrial PhD program at InterDigital, Inc., in collaboration with Inria, MimeTIC Research Team, on the topic of deep learning applied to character animation for content production.
Before my PhD, I graduated from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, EPFL (Bachelor of Science BSc, 2017 and Master of Science MSc, 2019) in Computer Science. I did my master thesis — Human Pose Estimation using Deep Generative Models — along with a six-month internship at Technicolor.
My research interests are deep learning and visual computing at large, and in particular computer graphics and computer vision. I also have a fascination for ray tracing and how it produces amazingly realistic images based on a single physics equation, the rendering equation.

About

I'm currently conducting a PhD program at Inria, Université de Rennes, CNRS, IRISA in collaboration with InterDigital, Inc., on the topic of deep learning applied to character animation for content production.
Before my PhD, I graduated from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, EPFL (Bachelor of Science B.Sc, 2017 and Master of Science M.Sc, 2019) in computer science. I did my master thesis — Human Pose Estimation using Deep Generative Models — along with a six-month internship at Technicolor, Rennes, France.
My research interests are deep learning and visual computing at large, and in particular computer graphics and computer vision. I also have a fascination for ray tracing and how it produces amazingly realistic images based on a single physics equation, the rendering equation.
